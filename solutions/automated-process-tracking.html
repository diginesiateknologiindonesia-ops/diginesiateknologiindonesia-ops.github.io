Solution Description

Automated Process Tracking solution enables companies to monitor and record every stage of the operational process automatically and in real-time. By relying on RFID technology, IoT, sensors, and integrated systems, this solution provides comprehensive visibility, high efficiency, and fast and accurate data-based decision making.

Key Features

  • Automatic tracking of workflow and manufacturing process flow
  • Integration with sensors, RFID gates, and control systems
  • Real-time monitoring of process status and environmental conditions
  • Condition/performance-based analytical dashboards & notifications
  • Integration with MES, ERP, and SCADA systems

Industrial Applications

  • Automated Manufacturing & Fabrication
  • Food & Beverage Processing
  • Pharmaceutical & Chemical
  • Automotive & Assembly Line
  • Distribution & Quality Control

Supporting Technology

UHF RFID Gate

Automatic detection of product and raw material movement between production processes.

IoT Sensors

Temperature, humidity, vibration, pressure sensors for real-time control of production conditions.

Chainway R3 / R6

Flexible desktop & handheld RFID Reader for integration at any process point.

Data Platform & Dashboard

Process visualization in one platform for centralized monitoring and control.

Benefits of the Solution

  • Increase operational efficiency and speed
  • Reduce manual intervention and risk of human error
  • Strengthen quality control and production standards
  • Provide transparency and automated process audit trails
