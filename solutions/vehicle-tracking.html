Solution Description

Vehicle Tracking Solutions enable companies to track and manage operational vehicles in real-time. With a combination of GPS technology, RFID, IoT sensors, and digital platforms, you can find out the location, speed, route, and condition of vehicles to improve logistics efficiency, safety, and overall fleet management.

Key Features

  • Real-time vehicle location tracking via GPS
  • Monitoring vehicle speed, direction, route & condition
  • Geofencing for vehicle boundary areas
  • Activity log and trip history
  • Automatic notification for abnormal events (overspeed, idle time, etc.)
  • Integration with RFID gate and access control systems

Usage Application

  • Distribution & Logistics
  • Public & Private Transportation
  • Fleet Management for service companies
  • Delivery of goods & food
  • Security Patrol & Operational vehicles

Supporting Technology

GPS Tracker Device

A small device mounted on a vehicle to track location and movement in real time.

RFID Vehicle Tags

Automatic identification of vehicles via RFID gates for access and location monitoring.

IoT Sensors

Temperature, fuel, door, or engine condition sensors that send data to a central platform.

Fleet Dashboard

Visualization and control platform for fleet management, alerting, and performance analytics.

Benefits of the Solution

  • Real-time vehicle tracking and monitoring
  • Savings on operational and fuel costs
  • Increase vehicle and asset security
  • Facilitate vehicle activity audits and reports
  • Support zero delay & zero loss policies
