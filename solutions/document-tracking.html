Solution Description

Document Tracking is a comprehensive solution for monitoring the movement, status, and history of both physical and digital documents. By leveraging RFID technology, barcodes, and integrated digital systems, this solution improves the security, efficiency, and transparency of document management across your organization.

Key Features

  • Automatic identification of documents using RFID tags or barcodes
  • Real-time tracking of document location and movement history
  • Automatic notification if a document is lost or out of the permitted area
  • Integration with digital document management systems (DMS)
  • User access logs for important documents
  • Dashboard and reporting on document usage or movement

Usage Application

  • Legal documents and contracts in law offices
  • Patient files and medical records in hospitals
  • Delivery notes and invoices in logistics companies
  • Tax and financial data archives
  • Audit and quality control documents

Supporting Technology

RFID & Barcode Tags

Attached to documents or folders for automatic identification and location tracking purposes.

RFID Scanner

RFID reader device for fast scanning of documents without opening or checking manuals.

Document Management System

Digital system for document version management, user access, and approval process automation.

Access Control Integration

Ensure that only specific personnel can access or move specific documents.

Benefits of the Solution

  • Reduce the risk of losing important documents
  • Improve the accuracy and efficiency of document management
  • Speed up the audit process and history tracking
  • Ensure compliance with information security policies
