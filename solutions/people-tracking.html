Solution Description

People Tracking enables real-time monitoring of the movement and presence of individuals within a specific area, improving operational efficiency, security, and space management. This solution can be used in office environments, factories, hospitals, schools, and other public areas.

Key Features

  • Monitoring the presence and movement of employees, visitors, or patients
  • Real-time tracking with RFID, BLE, GPS, or AI camera technology
  • Automatic notification for area or time violations
  • Integration with attendance and access control systems
  • Analysis of area density and movement for operational optimization

Usage Application

  • Hospitals - tracking patients, medical staff and equipment
  • Factories - monitoring worker presence and safety
  • Offices - integration with attendance and access control
  • Schools - monitoring student attendance and movement
  • Airports, malls, public places - visitor and congestion management

Supporting Technology

Wearable RFID / BLE Tags

Used by individuals to automatically detect location and movement.

RFID & BLE Reader

Installed at key points to scan tags and track movement paths.

AI-Powered CCTV

For intelligent presence detection, people counting, and crowd monitoring.

Dashboard Real-Time

Displays position, number of people in a certain area, and presence status.

Benefits of the Solution

  • Ensure the safety and security of people in the work area
  • Improve the accuracy of attendance and activity data
  • Support data-based decision making
  • Efficiency of space and facility management
