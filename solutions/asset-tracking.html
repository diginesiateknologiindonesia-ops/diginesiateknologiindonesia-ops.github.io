About Solutions

We provide Asset & Inventory Tracking solutions that help companies monitor assets and manage stock accurately, in real-time, and efficiently. With RFID, barcode technology, and cloud platforms, you can minimize asset loss, reduce human error, and improve operational efficiency.

Key Features

  • RFID & Barcode based tracking with cloud integration
  • Realtime location tracking and asset movement status
  • Automation of recording incoming, outgoing and audit stock
  • Comprehensive monitoring dashboard and analytical reports
  • Integration with ERP, WMS and other asset management systems

User Sector

  • Manufacturing & Industry
  • Retail & E-commerce
  • Warehouse & Logistics
  • Hospitals & Healthcare Facilities
  • Telecommunications & Utilities
