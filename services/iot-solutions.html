Overview

We provide IoT solutions based on sensors and real-time connectivity to accurately and efficiently monitor the movement of assets, people, vehicles, and environmental conditions. Our solutions are designed to help companies improve visibility, operational efficiency, and data-driven decision making.

Our Services

Real-time Location System (RTLS)

Track the movement of assets, vehicles, or people in real-time with UWB, BLE, or UHF RFID technology.

Environmental Monitoring

Automatic monitoring of temperature, humidity, air quality, vibration and other environmental parameters.

Smart Gateway & Sensor

Integration of IoT sensors with intelligent gateways based on MQTT, LoraWAN, or NB-IoT protocols.

IoT Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time data visualization through intelligent dashboards and reports for decision support.

Applications in Industry

  • Manufacturing: Monitoring machines and production environment
  • Warehouse & Logistics: Asset & pallet tracking, room temperature
  • Health: Patient monitoring and medical equipment
  • F&B & Pharmacy: Cold chain monitoring
  • Mining & Energy: Safety & environmental control
